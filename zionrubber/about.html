Zion Veneer Industries

Started under the visionary leardership of Mr. Shibu, Zion Industries was established in 1989 specialising in manufacturing high quality, durable rubber flooring mats for domestic, business, industrial and live stock use. Zion industries quickly grow into one of the premier manufacturer of Natural Rubber Products in Short Period of Time. Now with over 37 years of Experience, Zion has become one of the main Rubber Product Exporter in the Country.

Our Products

Our Products are our a Testement to our Commitment and Quality Control. Our Rubber Products are made from 100% Natural Rubber, Sourced from the Biggest Rubber Producing City in the World, Kottayam,Kerala. We currently Export to Over 5 Countries UK,USA,Germany, Italy, China. Not just Rubber Products, we have an Extensive Collection of EVA Form Products. All Over Products can be Customised in Customer Requirements.

World Class Machinery

At Zion Rubber, every product undergoes rigorous testing using our Digital Tensile Testing Machine. By measuring tensile strength, elongation, and material performance, we ensure our rubber mats deliver exceptional durability, safety, and reliability even under demanding conditions. Our commitment to quality control guarantees products you can trust for years of dependable service.
